CITY RULES

The mandatory guidelines for life within Grand Bangla City. Ignorance of these rules is not an excuse for misconduct.

Important Rules

Green Zones: Main Garage Only, Vehicle Shops, Hospitals, Restaurants & Company. You can't Harm, Shoot, Carry, Take-Hostage someone in Green Zones. No Toxicity & let people mind their own business. Park your Cars in Right manner & Keep out from using Horn/Burnout.

Other Zones: Blue Zone (Police Departments), Red Zone (Illegal processing factories).

Taking information outside the game & using it in RP situations is not allowed. Doing communication outside the game isn't allowed. Must use Phone or Radio.
Players must value their lives. You also should value your Vehicles, Items or Assets. Comply with demands of criminals for fear of retaliation (e.g., gun to your head).
You must not kill someone without a valid reason or giving them a reasonable ultimatum.
You can't kill/harm someone with any given reason with a vehicle.
You can't disconnect from a RP situation without completing it (Police chases, custody, etc.).

Other Rules

• Prohibits Hate Speech, Slang, confessional voice/text.

• No talking after death; must act injured.

• Prohibits rejoining situations for 15 minutes after death.

• No exploiting bugs, glitches, or real-money trading.

• No escaping to Green Zones or owned houses.

• Limit on participants: 4 for bank, 3 for shop, 6 for principal bank.

• Backup must be 2 KM away. No fake hostages.
